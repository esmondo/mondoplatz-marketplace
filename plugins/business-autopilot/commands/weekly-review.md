# Weekly Review

Generate a comprehensive weekly review of the business by gathering inputs from multiple agent perspectives.

## What this command does

Runs a coordinated check across your business operations:

1. **Product**: What shipped? What feedback came in? What's prioritized next?
2. **Marketing**: Content performance, engagement trends, pipeline status
3. **Operations**: Support volume, customer sentiment, any fires?
4. **Finance**: Cash position, revenue trend, notable expenses

## How to use

Provide any of the following:
- Links to analytics dashboards or data exports
- Notable events from the week
- Specific questions you want answered

If you don't provide data, this will generate a template of questions to answer.

## Output

You'll receive:
- One-page summary of the week
- Wins to celebrate
- Problems to address
- Key decisions needed
- Top 3 priorities for next week

## Best used on

Friday afternoon or Monday morning. Pick one and make it a habit.
